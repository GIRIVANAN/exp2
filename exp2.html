ABOUT

CIFF Chennai Film Festival is an international film festival Organised by the Indo Cine Appreciation Foundation (ICAF) in association with PVR and OneMercuri and supported by the Government of Tamil Nadu, the CIFF is scheduled to open with the Italian drama film Three Floors directed by Nanni Moretti

UPDATES

The 19th edition of the Chennai International Film Festival (CIFF), which will be held from December 30 to January 6, will have 121 films from 53 countries screened this year. , For the first time in the festival, films from Kazakhstan, Ghana, Cambodia, Uruguay and Latvia will be screened. The organising committee of CIFF announced on Thursday that 11 films have been shortlisted for the Tamil Film Competition by the preview committee. These are Ainthu Unarvugal, Boomika, Karnan, Kattil, Kayamai Kadakka, Maara, Ramae Aandalum Raavane Andaalum, Seththumann, Sivaranjiniyum Innum Sila Pengalum, Thaen , and Udanpirappae . “There will be six masterclasses organised through the duration of the festival. We want young talents to come and interact with experts from the film industry,” the organisers said. As a part of the Indian Panorama category, 10 films in Gujarati, Marathi, Kannada, Bengali, Telugu, and Dimasa are scheduled to be screened.
